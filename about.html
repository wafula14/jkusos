About Our club

The JKUSOS Club is a dynamic community dedicated to the study and application of statistics.

Our mission is to promote statistical literacy, Foster analytical thinking, and provode hands-on experience with real data challenges.

Our Goals

  • Promote the importance of statistics in various industries.
  • Organize educational events.
  • Encourage students to develop analytical and problem-solving skills.
  • Provide networking opportunities with professionals in the field.
